Slide 3 / Social Media Strategy

Social Media : Channel-Specific Tactics

INSTAGRAM (Community + Awareness)

Posts 2x/week : 30% educational (Ayurveda 101), 25% UGC testimonials, 20% lifestyle, 15% product, 10% behind-scenes | Stories daily | Reels 2x/week (trending format) | Hashtag strategy 20-30 researched tags | Community engagement (comment, like, reply daily)

TIKTOK (Discovery + Viral)

3-4 shorts/week | 30-60 sec format | Educational + entertainment ratio 50/50 | Trending sounds, challenges, formats | FYP-friendly (first 3 seconds critical) | Influencer collaborations for reach

PINTEREST (Traffic + SEO)

1 pin/day minimum | High-traffic keywords (Ayurveda, clean beauty, natural haircare, etc.) | Vertical format (1000x1500px) | Link to blog posts (SEO juice) | Group boards + niche community participation

EMAIL (Conversion + Loyalty)

Welcome automation (5-email nurture) | Weekly newsletter (storytelling + tips) | Post-purchase guides (routine education) | Abandoned cart recoveries | VIP/loyalty perks exclusive emails

Slide 4 / UX/UI Digital

UX/UI Website Recommendations

Feature 1 : Hair Quiz Diagnostic
Interactive questionnaire (5-7 questions) → personalized product recommendations. Increases engagement + conversion 15-20%

Feature 2 : Ritual Builder
User creates custom routine (2-4 products). Step-by-step guides, dosage recommendations, expected results timeline

Feature 3 : Community Hub
Customer gallery (before/afters), testimonial videos, product reviews. UGC social proof increases trust + conversion 20-30%

Feature 4 : Ingredient Library
Click any ingredient → learn biochimistry, benefits, sourcing story. Educational + transparency

Feature 5 : Blog Search Integration
Content searchable on site. Blog archive. Related article recommendations. Increases organic traffic + on-site time
