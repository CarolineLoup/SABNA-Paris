Slide 6 / Brand Personality

Visual Personality : Premium, Authentic, Accessible

✔ DO : Premium Minimalism

Clean layouts, white space, elegant typography, sophisticated imagery. No clutter, no cheapness

✔ DO : Authenticity First

Real ingredients, real people, real moments. Slightly imperfect = more human, more trustworthy

✔ DO : Warm + Inviting

Sage greens + warm beiges create cozy, ritual-ready feeling. Not cold, not sterile

✗ DON'T : Overly Exotic/Orientalist

Avoid stereotypical "spiritual" imagery (chakras, deities). Keep ayurveda respectful, modern

✗ DON'T : Corporate Sterility

Avoid stock photos, generic beauty imagery. No big smiles, no artificial perfection
