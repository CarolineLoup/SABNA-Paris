## SLIDE 10: PURCHASE DECISION DRIVERS

**Top 5 Factors** (ranked importance)

1. **Recommendation** (48%)
   - Friend: 68% of recs
   - Influencer: 22%
   - Expert: 10%

2. **Reviews/Avis** (42%)
   - TrustPilot/Google: 71%
   - Instagram comments: 19%
   - YouTube: 10%

3. **Social Proof** (35%)
   - "Seen on Instagram"
   - "Used by people I follow"

4. **Packaging/Aesthetic** (28%)
   - Visual appeal = purchase trigger
   - Premium feel = trust signal

5. **Price** (22%, surprisingly low)
   - Range acceptable: 15-35€
   - Premium OK if justified

## SLIDE 12: BARRIERS TO PURCHASE

**Primary Barriers**

1. **Price uncertainty** (45%)
   - "Why so expensive? No justification"
   - Need: Clear value communication

2. **Unknown brand** (52%)
   - "Never heard of it"
   - Need: Awareness building

3. **Efficacy doubt** (38%)
   - "Does it really work?"
   - Need: Clinical proofs

4. **Accessibility** (35%)
   - "Where to buy?"
   - Need: Distribution visibility

5. **Ingredient confusion** (28%)
   - "Is Neem actually good for hair?"
   - Need: Education content
